##Company Information
Antos Pinnacles (AP) is a team of passionate investment professionals with over 15 years of experience in both OTC and regulated financial markets. AP delivers professional trading-related services and cutting-edge trading infrastructure to both individual and institutional clients. Our mission is to equip clients with secure and accurate trading tools, including access to deep liquidity, enabling confident and efficient trading.
AP operates under a No Dealing Desk (NDD) execution model, where all client orders are routed directly to banks and ECN providers via aggregators or prime brokerages. In this NDD model, AP charges either a small trading commission or includes a minimal markup on spreads. To ensure lightning-fast trade execution, our Straight-Through Processing (STP) system sends client orders directly to liquidity providers connected to the interbank market.
